Soprano

Elena Brazhnik

Ukrainian soprano ELENA BRAZHNYK graduated with excellence from  the ‘National University of Culture and Arts’ (2007 A. Marhlevsky, N. Krechko) and ‘National Tschaikowsky Music Academy’ in Kiev, Ukraine (2012 E. Miroshnychenko, I. Semenenko). During her studies, she debuted in part Marfa from ‘Tsar’s Bride’, Rimski-Korsakov. In 2013 Elena Brazhnyk has participated in the master class of Vilma Vernocchi, Italy.  She is finalist of the International Singing Competition ‘Klaudia Taev’ Estonia (2015). From 2016 Elena Brazhnyk debuted in the Estonian National Opera and performed the leading role of Gilda (Verdi’s “Rigoletto”), Violetta Valery (Verdi’s “La Traviata”), Micaela (Bizet’s “Carmen”), La Contessa (Mozart “Le Nozze di Figaro”) Soprano part in Beethoven’s Mass in C-Dur. In 2019 Elena Brazhnyk performed the leading role of Marfa Rimsky-Korsakov’s “The Tsar’s Bride” in the new production of the Director Juriy Aleksandrov (St. Petersburg).
